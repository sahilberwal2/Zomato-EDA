# Zomato Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## Project Overview
This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Zomato Restaurant Dataset to uncover insights about restaurant ratings, cuisines, cities, countries, online delivery services, and customer engagement.

The objective is to understand customer preferences and restaurant trends using data visualization and statistical analysis.

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains information about restaurants listed on Zomato, including:

- Restaurant Name
- Country
- City
- Cuisines
- Aggregate Rating
- Rating Color
- Votes
- Average Cost for Two
- Online Delivery Availability
- Table Booking Availability

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze restaurant distribution across countries and cities.
- Study rating distributions and customer preferences.
- Identify the most popular cuisines.
- Examine online delivery and table booking trends.
- Analyze customer engagement through votes.
- Generate actionable business insights.

## Key Insights

### Country Analysis
- India contributes the majority of restaurants in the dataset.
- Other countries have significantly lower representation.

### Rating Analysis
- Most restaurants have ratings between 2.5 and 4.0.
- Highly rated restaurants are relatively rare.

### City Analysis
- New Delhi contains the highest number of listed restaurants.

### Cuisine Analysis
- North Indian cuisine is the most popular cuisine.
- Multi-cuisine restaurants are common.

### Online Delivery
- Online delivery is offered by only a subset of restaurants.
- Delivery services are concentrated in major cities.

### Votes Analysis
- Most restaurants receive a low number of votes.
- Restaurants with higher ratings generally attract more customer engagement.
